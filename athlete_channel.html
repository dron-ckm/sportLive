WHY I WANT TO PLAY

I grew up thinking that I would play in college. My mom was a scoring fiend at Colorado College who continues to play and coach to this day. I didn't really "want" to play at that level until I made the Virginia U-12 ODP team. I realized then that I had the capability to be a major impact player in my age group. It was the first opportunity where I saw my teammates as talented and capable instead

highlight of my sports career

In New Mexico, I was a high school, club and ODP player. The small population and big geography of New Mexico offered challenges to be a good player, a good teammate and leader far beyond what I would have gained staying in VA. As I return to Arlington to finish my last year and a half of high school, I am a more well-rounded player.
